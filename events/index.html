Events

For our internal events, we have General Body Meetings (GBM's) approximately once a month for announcements, fun activities, socializing, and most importantly, free food! Some other notable events include Gobblerfest at the beginning of the year, Mentor-Mentee Week in the Fall Semester, and our lovely SASE Gala hosted in the Spring Semester!

On our external events, we host professional gatherings such as workshops and information sessions throughout the year, most often during career fair season (at the beginning of the fall and spring semsesters). They are often great opportunities for networking, learning about different companies, and experience talking with recruiters.
