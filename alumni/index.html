Graduates: Class of 2019 and Before

Graduate School

Troy Kim graduated from Virginia Tech in 2019 and is currently in graduate school! He has interned at GE and US Department of Transportation in the past. Troy recommends attending SASE National Conference in order to make connections and improve your professional development skills.

General Motors

Jessica Shen currently works at General Motors and graduated from Virginia Tech in 2018! Jessica has interned at Dynetics and the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Lab. She recommends current underclassmen to apply to as many places as possible and to not let rejections get you down. Also, she suggests to only put things on your resume that you are willing to do!

Microsoft

Tony Tran is a Virignia Tech Alumni who currently works at Microsoft. He graduated from Tech in 2018 and had an internship at Northrop Grumman during his time at Tech. Tony recommends current undergraduates to not give up on finding an internship or full-time job and to utilize all of their resources, such as career fairs, applying online, and connections. "Eventually after many job rejections, at least one will end up as an offer."

Northrop Grumman

Arjun Balaji currently works at Northrop Grumman and graduted Virginia Tech in 2018. He has interned at GE Transportation and Northrop Grumman in the past! Arjun recommends current undergraduates to get involved with projects, design teams, and other clubs on campus.

Northrop Grumman

Syed Rumman graduated from Virginia Tech in 2017! Syed interned at Verizon and UTC Aerospace while he was still a undergraduate. Currently, he is working at Northrop Grumman. Some advice he would give to current underclassmen is to network as much as possible and to make sure your LinkedIn is polished!

Improbable

Brandon Nguyen graduated from Virginia Tech in 2017 and currently works at Improbable! He interned at the BIST Lab at Virginia Tech in the past and has also worked at National Instruments full-time.
